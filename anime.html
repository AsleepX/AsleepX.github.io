--- anime enthusiast

I started watching anime in middle school, and the first one I watched was "CODE GEASS Lelouch of the Rebellion". I took drawing lessons for a while and I post some of my artworks on my QQ space. There are drawings of characters from anime I love, as well as my own original characters. When real life becomes a bit exhausting, drawing a picture or watching a couple of episodes of my favorite anime can alleviate my stress and fatigue, rejuvenating me once again.
